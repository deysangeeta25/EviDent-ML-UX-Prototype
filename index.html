Welcome to the tool support for EviDent-ML

EviDent-ML is a multi-layered collaborative framework that involves multiple experts from diverse backgrounds and expertise to collaborate and engineer evidence-based data requirements for critical machine learning training. Such requirements can facilitate an evidence-driven assessment of the training data quality based on the experts' judgments about the satisfaction of the requirements.

Dempster Shafer's theory of evidence is applied to combine experts' subjective opinions about the quality of the collected training data. This tool support helps the users to follow the framework with the given tempaltes and easily calculate the final collective confidence of the experts in the fitness of the data.


Main Features

  • Assistance with the application of Dempster Shafer's Theory of Evidence
  • Reports on experts' belief masses for each data quality criteria
  • Download various Data Requirements Templates, Evidence Evaluation Template, and other guidelines
